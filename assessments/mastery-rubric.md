# Antigravity Mastery Rubric

Self-assessment to verify your understanding and capabilities.

---

## How to Use This Rubric

Rate yourself on each dimension:
- **0 - Not Started**: Haven't attempted
- **1 - Awareness**: Understand concept, haven't done it
- **2 - Attempted**: Tried it, still learning
- **3 - Competent**: Can do with reference materials
- **4 - Proficient**: Can do confidently
- **5 - Mastery**: Can teach others

**Passing Score**: Average of 3.0 or higher
